Latest Machinery Trending farm equipment, tools, and subsidies this season.

Latest Machines & Farm Equipment (2025)

  • Stubble Management: Happy seeder, super straw, balers (now subsidised in Punjab/Haryana).
  • Precision Seeders: Multi-crop planters, seed drills, rice-transplanters with higher efficiency.
  • Sprayers & Drones: Kisan drones for pesticide spraying, remote sensing, mapping.
  • Tillage & Land Prep: Power harrows, rotavators, disc ploughs, laser levellers.
  • Fruit & Veg: Cold chain mini units, solar dryers, packhouse machinery.
  • Regional subsidies: 30–75% off under SMAM, AIF, and state schemes.
  • How to Apply: District agri-office, online SMAM portal, or co-ops for demo and booking.

Contact your district agriculture office for eligibility, demonstration dates, and subsidy paperwork.

Major Govt Schemes for Farmers (2025)

  • PM-KISAN Samman Nidhi: ₹6000/yr in 3 installments, direct to bank account.
  • PMFBY: Crop insurance; KCC Credit Cards with up to ₹3 lakh, low interest.
  • Soil Health Card, RKVY-RAFTAAR, e-NAM for pan-India crop markets.
  • All schemes via banks, PACS, or official portals.

Loan & Insurance Schemes

  • PMFBY: Insurance, WBCIS: Weather, KCC: Loans, AIF: Grants for infra/storage.

Apply

Docs: Aadhaar, bank passbook, land record, crop details. Visit local bank/co-op/offices or online portals.
