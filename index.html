Latest Agricultural Machines & Government Schemes

  • Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM): Subsidies of 40–60% for tractors, power tillers, harvesters, Happy Seeder, Super Seeder, rotavator, zero-till drills, laser levelers, planters, sprayers, threshers, balers, and more.
  • Custom Hiring Centers (CHC): For farmers who can’t afford to buy, rent at low cost through government centers.
  • Happy Seeder & Super Seeder: Machines that cut and lift stubble, seed the new crop, and mulch straw to improve soil and reduce burning.
  • Zero-Till Seed Drills: Allow planting without ploughing, preserving soil structure/moisture.
  • Drone Technology: Expanding support for precision spraying, health monitoring, and field mapping.
  • Mini Processing Units: Mobile grading/cleaning and dryers for post-harvest use.
  • Eligibility: Most farmers, SHGs, women, SC/ST can apply for higher subsidies.
  • How to Apply: Use portals like agrimachinery.nic.in or respective state websites. Direct benefit transfer post-purchase/field verification.
